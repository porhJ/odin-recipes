Taco

These ground beef tacos are a nod to one of America favorite Mexican fast food chains —Taco Bell. I’m not much of a fast food fan, but Taco Bell tacos hit just right when it comes to drive-thru Mexican food.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • White onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato juice
  • Yellow cornmeal
  • Taco shells
  • Shredded cheese
  • Taco seasoning

Steps

  1. Brown the beef
  2. Sauté the onion and garlic. Once the meat is in smaller bits, stir in the onions and garlic and cook.
  3. Add the special seasonings.
  4. Toast your tacos. Use a sheet pan or a casserole dish to snuggle your corn tortillas together upright, then spoon the taco meat into them and sprinkle them with shredded cheese.
  5. Add the fixings. Now it’s time to dress these babies up.
